Church and wedding service

The service will take place in St Mary's Church in the village of Goudhurst.

The service starts 15:15. Anyone from abroad is encouraged to remember the timezone difference (I know I make that mistake all the time myself..).

Transportation

Guests without cars that are having trouble arranging transport are encouraged to get in touch and we'll try to advise and help out.

Dresscode

Come however you feel comfortable! If you're struggling and would like some guidelines I'd say it's hard to go wrong with a dark suit and tie for men and a dress or skirt for women.

Children

Very welcome in all ages! No one has ever died from a baby crying.
